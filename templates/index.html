FAQ

Where do the job listings come from?
All listings are from publicly available sources — LinkedIn, Indeed, ZipRecruiter, and company career pages — found in real-time when your application runs.
How do I know you actually applied?
You receive an email confirmation for every application, including job title, company, and listing link. You can also verify by checking your LinkedIn "Applied Jobs" page — every application we submit appears there.
Do you guarantee I'll get a job?
No. We guarantee we submit the number of applications you purchased to real, currently-open job listings. Employers make hiring decisions — but applying to 150 jobs gives you dramatically better odds than 5.
Can I get a refund?
All sales are final. You're paying us to submit real applications to real job listings, which we do. You can verify every submission.
Is this allowed by LinkedIn?
ApplyBot uses browser automation to fill out LinkedIn Easy Apply forms — the same forms you'd fill manually, just faster. We recommend spacing out applications to stay within normal usage patterns.
ApplyBot is an automation tool, not a job placement agency. We submit applications to publicly listed jobs using the information you provide. We do not guarantee interviews, job offers, or employment. We are not affiliated with LinkedIn, Indeed, or any employer. Your personal information is used only to complete the applications you've purchased.
