Quick Answers

Q: Where do you apply?
LinkedIn (Easy Apply), USAJobs.gov, and public job boards. We focus on platforms where applications can be submitted programmatically.

Q: How long does it take?
Applications begin within 24 hours. A batch of 50 typically completes in 2–3 days depending on match volume.

Q: Can I target specific companies?
We match by job title, category, and location. For company-specific targeting, email us after purchase.

Q: What if I don't get interviews?
We submit applications — we can't guarantee interviews. But our targeting uses real job data and BLS statistics to maximize matches. Paying for the application service, not a guaranteed outcome.
