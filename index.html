FAQ

Where do the job listings come from?
All listings are from publicly available sources — LinkedIn, Indeed, ZipRecruiter, USAJobs, and company career pages — found in real-time when your application runs.
How do I know you actually applied?
You receive an email confirmation for every application, including job title, company, and listing link.
Do you guarantee I'll get a job?
No. We guarantee we submit the number of applications you purchased to real, currently-open job listings. Applying to 150 jobs gives you dramatically better odds than 5.
