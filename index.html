FAQ

Where do the job listings come from?
All listings are from publicly available sources — LinkedIn, Indeed, ZipRecruiter, USAJobs, and company career pages. Counts are based on real market data from BLS and job board APIs.
How do I know you actually applied?
You receive an email confirmation for every application, including job title, company, and link.
Do you guarantee I'll get a job?
No. We guarantee we submit the number of applications you purchased. Hiring decisions are made by employers.
Can I get a refund?
All sales are final. You're paying us to submit applications. If a technical issue prevents delivery, contact us.
Do I need a resume?
Nope. We have a built-in resume builder — just fill out your experience and we'll format it professionally.
ApplyBot is an automation tool, not a job placement agency. We submit applications to publicly listed jobs. We do not guarantee interviews, job offers, or employment. Not affiliated with LinkedIn, Indeed, or any employer. Your data is used only to complete your purchased applications.
