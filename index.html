Quick Answers

Where do you apply on my behalf?
LinkedIn (Easy Apply listings), USAJobs.gov (federal positions), and public job boards. We focus on platforms where applications can be submitted programmatically with accurate data from your resume.
How long does a batch take?
Applications begin within 24 hours of purchase. A batch of 50 typically completes in 2–3 days depending on match volume in your target area.
Can I target specific companies?
We match by job title, category, and location. For company-specific targeting, contact us after your purchase and we'll prioritize those employers.
What if I don't get interviews?
We submit applications — we cannot guarantee interviews or offers. Our targeting uses real BLS job data to maximize match volume. You're paying for the submission service, not a guaranteed outcome.
