Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

ApplyBot ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information you provide:

  • Resume data: Your work history, education, skills, and contact information as provided in your resume or resume builder.
  • Job preferences: Target job titles, locations, and categories you select.
  • Contact information: Your email address (collected via Stripe during payment).
  • Payment information: Processed entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your credit card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Submit job applications on your behalf to publicly listed positions.
  • Send you email confirmations for each application submitted.
  • Provide customer support if you contact us.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your resume and contact information are used solely to complete the applications you have purchased. Information is shared only with the employers to whom you have authorized us to apply.

4. Data Retention

We retain your resume, application history, and contact information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing support@applybot.ai. Application records submitted to employers cannot be retracted from those employers' systems.

5. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission. Payment processing is handled by Stripe, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified provider. Our infrastructure runs on Render, which provides physical and network security.

6. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies or analytics. Stripe may set cookies necessary for payment processing.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of communications

Contact support@applybot.ai for any privacy requests.
