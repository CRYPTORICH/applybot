Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use ApplyBot's resume builder, we collect the information you voluntarily provide: name, email address, phone number, location, professional summary, work experience, skills, and education history. We do not collect any information beyond what you type into the form.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to generate your resume and, if you purchase a plan, to submit job applications on your behalf. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

If you provide your email address, we may send you a copy of your generated resume and, only if you purchase a plan, application confirmation emails. We do not send marketing emails without your explicit consent.

3. Data Storage

Resume data is processed in your browser and transmitted to our secure servers only when you unlock your resume or purchase a plan. We use industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3) for all data transmission. Resume data is stored temporarily and may be deleted after 30 days of inactivity.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

ApplyBot uses browser localStorage to save your email address (if provided) for your convenience on return visits. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies. The only third-party services we use are Stripe (for payment processing) and Leaflet/OpenStreetMap (for the job density map).

5. Third-Party Services

Stripe: When you make a purchase, your payment information is processed directly by Stripe. We never see or store your full credit card details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data: stripe.com/privacy.

ZipRecruiter: Our sponsor offers link to ZipRecruiter, a separate service. We are not responsible for their data practices.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@applybot.ai. We will respond within 30 days. California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected and to opt out of its sale (though we do not sell data).

7. Children's Privacy

ApplyBot is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: privacy@applybot.ai
