💡 Why We Started This

The original thesis. March 2026.
🏪

Shops were invisible

Local smoke shops and corner stores had no digital loyalty. No CRM. No way to track customers. They ran on paper punch cards in 2026.

👥

Customers wanted rewards

Every other industry has points. Coffee shops, airlines, grocery. But local shops? Nothing. Customers were ready to switch for a better experience.

📊

Data was the real product

Purchase-verified customer data is worth 10x more than Facebook's inferred data. Nobody had it because nobody had the POS integration. We built it.

💰

Everybody eats

Shops get free loyalty software. Customers get rewards. Advertisers get verified targeting. Platform takes 30% of ad revenue. Shops keep 70%. Zero cost to join.

ApplyBot was the wedge

238 local business sites rank on Google. Free resume builder brings job seekers. CRM tracks every call. The pipeline feeds directly into RacksRewards onboarding.

🚀

Zero customer acquisition cost

ApplyBot's SEO sites rank locally. Job seekers find us organically. Shops get pitched by our CRM. We never paid for a single lead.
